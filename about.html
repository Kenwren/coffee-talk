Where to begin?

25 years ago, we started a small coffee business in Hokkaido Japan. Out in the snowy prefecture, like living in Winnipeg Canada. The coffee is packaged in vapor-deposited aluminum bags with a high barrier to prevent moisture. The bag is filled with nitrogen gas to prevent oxidation. The aroma and taste of roasting can be enjoyed when the seal is opened.

That was then.

Fast forward to today, its still basically the same. But still, it is a place and time where anything can happen. The aroma of espresso beans grinding to make a prefect cup of coffee. People started to talk about their lives. it is a cafe that holds the many dreams and stories of its people.
